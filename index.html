About Me

About me: I am Sley. I like to go by nicknames every now and then because I don’t like taking myself too seriously. I can be professional when the time comes, but I will always be a little girl at heart. Sley was a nickname given to me from friends in the military. It’s a play on my last name. Los Angeles is where I call home.

I am someone who likes to engage in conversation and have a meaningful experience with someone as we discuss life. I don’t like hearing myself talk, I like hearing what’s going on in your head. I take my hobbies seriously and they define me. I love drawing and making digital art on my iPad. I’ve been drawing since kindergarten. I love hitting the batting cages as baseball is my favorite spot. Being outside and being active is a must for me. Researching and knowing the why’s to every obscure topic is a hobby of mine. I need to research everything that pops into my head, I am a very curious person. My favorite food is shrimp and I prefer it in an asian dish. I have a deep love and empathy for women. Some goals are to build a successful life, career and eventually start a family.
